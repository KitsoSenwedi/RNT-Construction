About Company.

RNT construction is a construction company specializing in various sectors in the construction industry. Our motto "Together we stand" demonstrates the pride we have in unity and building a solid foundation together. We have a dedicated team with unique offerings and capabilities waiting to tackle our clients problems.





What we do
We deliver all kinds of civil construction and building services such as renovations, house plans, plumbing and various building maintenace issues. We are always glad to give a helping hand where ever its due, with our 24 hour emergency services. No job is too big nor too small as we always take challenges and bring them to a conclusion.





RNT construction is committed to Broad-Based black Economic Empowerment(B-BBEE) and is proud to be a level one contributor.
